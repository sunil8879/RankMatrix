Privacy Statement

1. Our Commitment to Anonymity

RANKMATRIX is built on the principle of "Privacy by Design." We believe that your academic and career research should remain entirely confidential. Unlike other educational platforms, we do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in via social media to access our 215+ meta-ranking JSON databases. Your search history and the universities you compare stay on your device, not on our servers.

2. Technical Data Collection

To ensure the stability of our Rank Matrix and to prevent automated bot attacks, our servers collect basic, non-identifiable technical logs. This includes your IP address, browser type, and time-stamps of data requests. This information is used strictly for server optimization and is automatically purged every 30 days. we do not use this data to profile users or serve targeted advertisements.

3. Local Processing

When you use the "Check Meta Score" or "USA Salary Dataset" tools, the filtering logic happens locally on your computer using JavaScript. Your selections (such as specific degree levels or course names) are never transmitted to a central database for storage. This ensures that your financial and career interests are never "leaked" to third-party marketing firms or university recruiters.

4. Third-Party Links & Cookies

Our website contains links to external ranking bodies such as topuniversities.com and shanghairanking.com. We do not have control over their privacy practices. We use only "Essential Cookies"—tiny files that remember your subject selection so the rank cards at the top of the page stay in sync as you scroll. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party analytical cookies that follow you across the web.
